Welcome to FST Auto Service

FST Auto Service is an authorised Bosch Car Service workshop, providing a top standard of quality for a service. With our wide range of expert services, we can offer everything you could possibly need for your vehicle - from inspection, service and repairs right through to customised retrofitting work. We can ensure no loss of manufacturer's warranty coverage for your vehicle.


The partnership between FST Auto Service and Bosch Car Service began in December 2012. FST Auto Service caught Bosch Car Service's attention with how it conducts itself as an entity which lives by the core values integrity, reliability, honesty, and fairness.


We approach everyday with curiosity, looking for new and better ways to provide our services to customers. We believe that a happy customer is a returning customer, that is why we ensure that anyone that leaves our premises leaves with a smile on their face. If a customer is uhappy with our service we will make it our personal mission to attend to that issue and if needs be, we'll have our whole technical staff attending to your car - you are that important to us.

Our Mission
To put a smile on a customers face - we don't care about the money, anyone can make money but only a few people can put a smile on someone's, and that's our personal mission.

There's no greater feeling than when a customer leaves our premises with a smile on their face, looking into our eyes and saying the words "Thank you".
Our Vision
To become a company that cares more about its customers, and forming lifelong relationships. To drop our customers jaws through a high quality of service.
